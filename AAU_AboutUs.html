AARHUS ARTISTIC UNDERGROUND

The gems hidden in plain sight

On a mission to bring people and art closer

What is this place? Simple! It is a new way of finding hidden art gems around Aarhus! Not all art expositions are museums! Do you feel like you already saw all there in the city? Do you wish for way to find some amazing places hidden in plain sight? This is what we are for!

We are a team of artistically driven individuals looking to help nourish people's cultural knowledge. Our process is straightforward: we hear about a new possible location to add to the website, we research, we create content and bring it to the eyes of the world in a fun easy-to-read format!
